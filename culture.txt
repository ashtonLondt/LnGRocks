1. Values and Mission: A strong company culture often starts with a clear set of values and a mission statement that employees can rally behind. These define the company's identity and purpose.

2. Communication: Open and transparent communication is crucial. A culture that encourages honest dialogue and feedback fosters trust and collaboration among team members.

3. Employee Engagement: A positive culture promotes high levels of employee engagement. This can be encouraged through opportunities for professional development, recognition of achievements, and a supportive work environment.

4. Work-Life Balance: A healthy culture values work-life balance and promotes well-being among employees. This can include flexible work arrangements, wellness programs, and policies that support mental health.

5. Team Dynamics: Cultures that foster strong team dynamics encourage collaboration and cooperation. Team-building activities and shared goals can strengthen these relationships.
